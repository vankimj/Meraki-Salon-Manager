## Tracking

Every outreach attempt + conversation + demo + follow-up should land in a single tracking sheet. Recommended fields are listed at the bottom of each doc. A simple Google Sheet works — you don't need a CRM yet.

## Voicing rules (so it sounds like you, not like a corporate template)

- **Peer-to-peer.** You own a salon. They own a salon. Skip vendor language.
- **Specific over general.** Every email/script references at least one concrete thing about the recipient.
- **Honest.** If they're a bad fit, say so kindly. Don't pretend.
- **Easy yes / easy no.** One clear next step. No "let me know what you think."
- **Short.** A great email is 5-7 sentences. Anything longer = lower reply rate.

## When to update these docs

- After every batch of 5-10 conversations: refresh the question bank with new patterns
- After every batch of 10 outreach attempts: refresh the recruitment scripts based on what's converting
- After 25 cohort applications: refresh the intake form fields based on what info you actually use vs ignore
- After 30 demos: refresh the post-demo emails based on which variants close

These aren't static templates — they're a living playbook.
